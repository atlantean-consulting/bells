# Ships Bell

This app rings a traditional ship's bell at every half-hour mark, keeping time the way sailors have for centuries. The bells serve a practical purpose as well: if you know what part of the day it is (say, "morning"), you can count the bells and know what time it is without having to look away from what you're doing.

## Two Watch Systems
Choose between the traditional Royal Navy system (seven watches per day with dogged watches) or the experimental Atlantic Republic Navy system (four six-hour watches with extended two-tone bell patterns).

## Configurable Crew Rotation
Name your teams and set a rotation according to your schedule. The app tracks which team has the watch at any moment.

## Night Vision Dark Mode
Dark mode uses red-on-black to protect your night vision when you're underway. Light mode rocks the Green and Gold.

## Quiet Hours
Set quiet hours so the bells don't ring while you're in your rack. Enabled by default from 2200 to 0600. Note: If you want the bells to ring before activating quiet mode, change the times to 2201 and/or 0559.

## Respects Your Device
Bells play on the notification audio stream, so they follow your device volume and honor "Do Not Disturb" mode. Minimal battery usage — the app sleeps between bells.

## Messages of the Day
A rotating selection of messages displayed on the main screen. Fully customizable. MOTDs rotate at midnight local time.
