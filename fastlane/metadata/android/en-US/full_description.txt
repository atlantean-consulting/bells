Ships Bell strikes the traditional ship's bell at every half-hour mark, keeping time the way sailors have for centuries.

<b>Two Watch Systems</b>
Choose between the traditional Royal Navy system (seven watches per day with dogged watches) or the experimental Atlantic Republic Navy system (four six-hour watches with extended two-tone bell patterns).

<b>Configurable Crew Rotation</b>
Name your teams, set a rotation anchor from the ship's log, and the app tracks which team has the watch at any moment.

<b>Night Vision Dark Mode</b>
Dark mode uses red-on-black to protect your night vision when you're underway. Light mode flies the green and gold.

<b>Quiet Hours</b>
Set quiet hours so the bells stand down while you sleep alongside. Enabled by default from 2200 to 0600.

<b>Respects Your Device</b>
Bells play on the notification audio stream, so they follow your device volume and honor Do Not Disturb mode. Minimal battery usage — the app sleeps between bells.

<b>Messages of the Day</b>
A rotating selection of messages displayed on the main screen. Fully customizable.

No ads. No tracking. No network access. Just bells.
